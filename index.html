About The Festival

Built for big singalongs, easy afternoons and one more song.

ESCAPE is a community-first music festival now heading into its third year. The first edition took place on 28 March 2025, followed by the second on 28 February 2026. The next event will take place at Coconut Park 2 Koh Norea, bringing together families, friends, local talent, international acts and DJs across two stages.
